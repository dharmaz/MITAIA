Six Forces Converging Now

Healthcare faces a perfect storm — and AI is the only scalable response.

An R&D productivity crisis, a demographic time bomb, unsustainable costs, workforce collapse, a data explosion, and accelerating regulation are colliding simultaneously. No previous generation faced all six at once.

AI doesn't just help — it is the only path to maintaining quality care, accelerating drug discovery, and building financially sustainable health systems for the decades ahead.

Leadership Roadmap

Six Imperatives — Starting Now

A phased action plan for healthcare leaders over the next 24 months.

  • 01
    Build the Data Foundation Assess EHR interoperability, establish HIPAA-compliant data sharing, and develop a real-world evidence strategy. A FAIR-compliant data estate is the highest-leverage investment you can make.
  • 02
    Establish AI Governance Now Model validation, bias monitoring, and human oversight policies. FDA dual AI councils signal hardening expectations — start minimum viable governance today.
  • 03
    Define Your SaMD Strategy Pre-Submission meetings before product roadmaps lock. Align regulatory pathways with AI lifecycle needs — regulatory clarity is a competitive advantage.
  • 04
    Shape — Don't Just Comply Engage FDA/EMA on GxP AI frameworks proactively. Appoint an executive AI sponsor with board-level mandate and invest in workforce reskilling.
  • 05
    Move from Pilots to Scale 52% report moderate ROI; 30% report high ROI. 45% of GenAI users saw returns within 12 months. Competitors are scaling — inaction is the real risk.
  • 06
    Embed Equity in Every AI Solution Validate AI in underserved populations. Algorithmic fairness audits as standard practice. Diverse training data as a regulatory prerequisite.
